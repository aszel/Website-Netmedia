Research group radio technology

<<< back to activities

The Research group for radio technology is located on the campus "Allee der Kosmonauten" in Berlin. Momentarily it consists of one scientist, 5 students, graduands and the leader of the group.

The team works in different laboratories for digital electronis, microwaves and different measuring methods.

The researching group is part of the Center of Excellence for Media, Nets und Mobile Computing.

The main focal points are:

  • Signal processing in communication
  • Wireless data transmission
  • Radio networks

For further informationen please visit the website of the researching group.
