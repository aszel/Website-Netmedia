Research group Inka

<<< back to activities

We are implementing highly application-oriented software solutions, including several base modules and products for information-sharing and communication purposes. Of course, research aspects dealing with these complex topics contain a vast amount of challenges.

For this reason we are engaged in branches like developing algorithms and processing digital data as well as designing interfaces and enhancing usability. Additionally, there is also a strong research focus on optimizing processes in the development of multimedia applications.

The researching group is part of the Center of Excellence for Media, Nets und Mobile Computing.

Major topics we are concentrating on are:

  • Mobile information systems
  • eLearning solutions (virtual teaching and learning)

For further informationen please visit the website of the researching group.
