Center of Excellence
Media, Nets and Mobile Computing

at the FHTW Berlin

The centre of Excellence, media, net and mobile Computing maintains the interdisciplinary collaboration with a huge number of university-internal and external partners, under it research institutes, colleges at home and abroad as well as industrial enterprises.

The field of work of this centre of excellence is:

  • Net based systems
  • Wireless transmission technology
  • Sensor-based radio networks
  • Model-based electronic design (FPGA)
  • Informatics in media
  • Video technology / video coding / video communication
  • Multimedia production / MM information systems
  • Signal processing in communication systems / telelearning / teleteaching
