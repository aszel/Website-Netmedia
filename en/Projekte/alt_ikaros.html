Mobile Computing - Datenbanken, Informationssysteme und mobile Anwendungen

In the main focus of the research projects were the development of base modules and base services for mobile systems, with priority for notebooks, Tablet PCs, PDAs and mobile phones of the third generation. The base modules should be adaptable after a parametrisation easily on different areas of application. The system was realised with Open Source ideas and is available for research facilities and enterprises to the advancement, complement and adaptation. Exemplarily applications were built up for the following areas: - Fair and exhibit information and -service systems, -real estate service systems, -Facility Management, -culture/museum, -etourism, -building site management- and -information systems and -automatization technologies.
