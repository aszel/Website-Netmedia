DABARE - DAtenBAnkbasiertes ReEngeneering

The aim was to offer the possibility of re-use of big software systems. This contain the re-use of single modules or functional partial components as well as the data base. Building up on the successful identification of components as well as the linked data from software systems the development of new or modified systems is possible. This is reached due to the integration of modern components which causes changes of the functionality of single modules and the migration of the relational data bases. In addition, new developed technologies should simplify the optimisation of old software systems.
