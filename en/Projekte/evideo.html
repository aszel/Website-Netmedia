Game-based eVideo (ESF-Project)

Content

We offer an online research project which discusses, develops and evaluates modern teaching-/learning scenarios for the education and continuing education, under use of innovative audio-/video technology and game-based narrative structures or motivation factors (e.g. in virtual worlds like Second Life, in the presence apprenticeship with media support, in the online apprenticeship with web 2.0 and for mobile Learning).

  • Overview about topical discussions and developments in the serious games- and video technology area
  • Possibility of discussion possibility with professional experts in games- and video area
  • Knowledge of topical information technologies and communication technologies (Web 2.0 & Social Software)
  • Active usage of the modern FHTW-infrastructure (learning platform, video conferences
  • solutions, wiki, labour etc.) - Construction of an individual, relevant architecture of information which can serve as a basis for structured lifelong learning
  • Development of absolutely new teaching and learning scenarios in presented, interdisciplinary teamwork leads if necessary to new fields of work, cooperations and subsequent projects (Learning 2.0)
  • Exchange and interlinking with project-cooperation partners - knowledge of modern presentation forms of structured results and real test and realisation of different presentation technologies (online as offline) - construction of an individual, relevant architecture of information which can serve as a basis for structured lifelong learning
  • Instructions to a modern online identity or business card

Target audience

Innovative, eager with research, video and or games and or design and or weave 2.0-and or teaching-oriented and learning-oriented employees, job-seekers and students who have interest in a modern continuing education in an open university sphere with optimised basic conditions.

Methodology

Blended Learning draught as a work accompanying mix from Virtual Classroom components, collaborative online study, study groups-based research projects and real life workshops.

Certificate

As an end a certificate can be acquired beside the certificate of attendance (for 1 semester) if both semesters were finished successfully.
