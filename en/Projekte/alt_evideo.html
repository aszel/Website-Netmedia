E-Video (ESF Project)

Description

Contents of the project were the wide area of Video and eLearning. In eVideoLab of the FHTW Berlin standardize able storyboards and work flows were developed for modern teaching scenarios. These scenarios were tested in a continuing education course which delivers important evaluation results.

During the conveyor phase three on-line continuing education courses were realised which indicated application methods and application examples of videos in modern eLearning scenarios and provided the practical Handling of the digital video technology. Purpose was it to show the participant the range in technical like didactic possibilities of video provided information.

The course itself consisted of theoretical and practical, modular unities: Conceived as a Blended Learning course with a theoretical on-line part and a workshop oriented presence part the plan used the personnel, technical and spatial infrastructure of the FHTW Berlin.
