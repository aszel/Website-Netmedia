eTrain

further informations...
Electronic, internetbased learning infrastructere for further education courses at the FHTW Berlin.
Etrain consists of 6 sub-projects with individuel leaders. The centre of cooperation "Wissenschaft - Praxis" takes care about the complete coordination of the whole project.

Running time: 1. Juni 2003 - 31. Mai 2006

MediaLabs: sub-projekts

Advancement of the infrastructere for further education courses within the sub-projects "Video on Demand" and "Ferngesteuerte Laborversuche".
For a video-on-demand-service a server has been set up and fitted with teaching material on video. Further that a remote-controlled measuring station has been assembled. Together with according teaching material the practical part of a lecture can be covered.

Running time: 12.1999 - 10.2001

Waveletbased interference signal elimination

Algorithms were developed for suppression of interfering signals in digital pictures. With help of this technique it is able to remove so called noise in images. The objective and visual results are very good in comparison with other methods. The complexity of the software is so low that it can be used in real time picture refinement.

Running time: 12.1999 - 12.2000

Musical / Notebookuniversity

further informations...
In the subprojekt "Mobile Videokonferenzen" a video conference system was installed that can be used on the whole campus of the FHTW. Blended eLearning-Kurs (ESF Project)

further informations...
A internet-based course was developed and accomplished to teach the participants how to use different modern digital audio- and video-technologies. The aim was to enable the participants to face problems in the digital society. All members were allowed to follow the lectures asynchronously over the internet or beeing present. The must of physical presence was kept on a absolut minimum.
