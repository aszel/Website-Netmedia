Mobile Computing - Datenbanken, information systems and mobile applications

The further education course assumes a classical education in data bank technology. With among other things the data bank modelling as well as program technologies with the conversion of data banks will be learnt and varied applications will be introduced. In parallel the course deals intensely and practically oriented with the information systems whose structure and components from are analysed conceptual and technically. Further that mobile applications are analysed, modelled and developed. The course is complemented with the integration of modules to multimedia, mobile devices and network technologies. For the continuing education course a high job market inquiry exists. Data bank systems and mobile information technologies have become key technologies which are used as a core technology in all professional systems of information, goods landlord shaft systems and administrative systems, E and m Commerce applications and process processing systems. The career prospects are very good because the need and also the claim will grow to such systems, especially mobile radio systems of the third and fourth generation.
